## ✨ Key Features

- 🕌 **Prayers & Supplications** — Full audio & text library of Du'as and Ziyarat
- 📿 **Dhikr Counter (سجده شمار)** — Digital tasbih with smart tracking
- 🧭 **Qibla Finder** — Accurate Sharia-compliant Qibla direction
- ⏰ **Prayer Times (اوقات شرعی)** — Location-based daily prayer times
- 📖 **Religious Etiquette (آداب)** — Guidelines for every occasion
- 🎵 **Smart Audio Player** — Stream & download prayers offline
- 📅 **Daily Adhkar** — Morning and evening remembrances
- 🌙 **Mahdism & Spirituality** — Content on awaiting the Imam
- 📴 **Offline Support** — Fully usable without internet
